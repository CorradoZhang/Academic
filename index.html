About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Galway. My research focuses on reinforcement learning and evolutionary computing.

I hold dual bachelor’s degrees in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics, and earned an MSc in Artificial Intelligence with First Class Honours.

My current work explores adaptive policy learning and hybrid evolutionary algorithms for complex decision-making problems, with applications in healthcare and optimization.
